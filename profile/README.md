# UQ Open Source Notes

## Introduction
This organisation is dedicated to providing well formed, informative course notes to all students at UQ. The notes are written in typst and compiled to pdf, and are available for free on our website at https://uqosnotes.github.io/. 
The notes are also available on GitHub at the repo https://github.com/UQOSNotes/notes/, where you can contribute to the notes if you have content to share. 
The front-end for the website is also open source and can be found at https://github.com/UQOSNotes/uqosnotes.github.io/.

## Contributing or Joining
If you would like to contribute to the notes, or join the organisation, please feel free to submit a pull request with your changes, or reach out to us through our contact page on the website. We welcome contributions that improve the content, organization, or formatting of the notes. Please ensure that any changes you make are consistent with the overall style and format of the notes, and that they do not introduce any errors or issues. AI generated contributions are welcome, but *please* ensure you review the content generated, as AI can produce innaccurate, or incorrect content.
